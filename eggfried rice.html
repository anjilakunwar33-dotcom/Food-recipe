Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Remove and set aside.
  2. Add onion and carrot, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and soy sauce, mix well.
  4. Add scrambled eggs back to the rice and combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.
