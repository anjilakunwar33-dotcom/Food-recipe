Chicken Choila

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil the chicken until cooked, then drain the water.
  2. Mix chicken with mustard oil, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, garlic paste, ginger paste, and salt.
  3. Marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. Grill or pan-fry until cooked and slightly crispy.
  5. Add chopped green chilies and mix well.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with beaten rice.
