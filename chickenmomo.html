Chicken Momo

Ingredients

  • 250g minced chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Momo wrappers (store-bought or homemade)

Instructions

  1. Mix minced chicken with onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, chili, salt, and pepper.
  2. Take a momo wrapper, place a spoonful of filling in the center, and fold/seal properly.
  3. Steam the momos for 10-12 minutes until cooked.
  4. Serve hot with achar (Nepali dipping sauce) or tomato chutney.
